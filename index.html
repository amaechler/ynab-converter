YNAB CSV Converter

Convert your bank CSV to YNAB-compatible format

How it works

  1. Select your bank's CSV export file
  2. Click "Convert to YNAB Format"
  3. Your converted file will download automatically

Expected Input Format

Your CSV should have these columns:

transaction_date, post_date, type, details, amount, currency

Output Format

YNAB Format 2:

Date, Payee, Memo, Amount
Note: This converter flips the sign of amounts to match YNAB convention (positive = inflow, negative = outflow) and converts dates to MM/DD/YYYY format.

All conversion happens in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server.
